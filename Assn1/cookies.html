Wasabi Cookies

Ingredients Amount
Eggs 4
Flour 3 cups
Butter 1/4 pound
Salt 2 tbs
Wasabi 1 cup

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Add egg, flour, butter, wasabi, and salt into a bowl and mix untill fully blended
  3. Drop tablespoon size amounts of batter on a baking sheet with some distance between them
  4. Bake for 30 minutes
  5. Let stand for 15 minutes

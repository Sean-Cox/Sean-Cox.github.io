BBQ Cake

Ingredients Amount
Eggs 8
Flour 8 cups
Butter 1 pound
Salt 2 tbs
BBQ Sauce 4 cup

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Add egg, flour, butter, salt, and bbq sauce into a bowl and mix untill fully blended
  3. Pour entire mixture into a cake tin and place in the oven
  4. Bake for 45 minutes
  5. Remove from oven and let stand for 15 minutes

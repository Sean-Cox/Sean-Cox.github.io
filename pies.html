Cinnamon Apple Pie

Ingredients Amount
Pie Crust 1
Apples 12
Cinnamon 4 tbs
Vanilla 2 tbs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Core apples and cut them into sections of desired thickness
  3. Place chopped apples, vanila, and cinnamon in a bowl
  4. Gently mix ingredients together
  5. Add mixture into pie crust and smooth mixture flat
  6. Bake for 45 minutes
  7. Remove from oven and let stand for 15 minutes

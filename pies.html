Terrible Treats

Desserts For Your Worst Enemies

Home Cookies Cakes Pies
Ingredient Name Amount
pie crust 1
apples 12
cinamon 4 tbs
vanilla 2 tbs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees ferenheit
  2. Core apples and cut them into sections of desired thickness
  3. Place chopped apples, vannila, and cinamon in a bowl
  4. Gently mix ingredients together so that not to bruise that apples
  5. Add mixture into pie crust and smooth mixture flat
  6. Bake for 45 minutes
  7. Remove from oven and let stand for 15 minutes

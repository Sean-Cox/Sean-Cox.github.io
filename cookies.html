Pies

Ingredient Name Amount
eggs 4
flour 3 cups
butter 1/4 pound
salt 2 tbs
wassabi 1 cup

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees ferenheit
  2. Add egg, flour, butter, and salt into a mixing bowl and mix untill fully blended
  3. Lay out tablespoon size amounts of batter on a baking sheet with some distance between them
  4. Use a fork to flatten the tops of the raw cookies
  5. Spread wassabi over the ridges of the cookies (try to make it look like icing)
  6. Bake for 30 minutes
  7. Let stand for 15 minutes

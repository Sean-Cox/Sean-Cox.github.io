Cakes

Pies

Ingredient Name Amount
eggs 8
flour 8 cups
butter 1 pound
salt 2 tbs
bbq sauce 4 cup

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees ferenheit
  2. Add egg, flour, butter, salt, and bbq sauce into a mixing bowl and mix untill fully blended
  3. Pour entire mixture into a cake tin and place in the oven
  4. Bake for 45 minutes
  5. Remove from oven and let stand for 15 minutes
